Home


Welcome to TYMO! TYMO is a free service that is designed to help people manage their time both effieciently and in a timely fashion. TYMO can use factors about upcoming projects and a schedule to help determine when you should study.


About

TYMO was started by four teenagers at TeenHacksLI who wanted to find a way to help people manage their time beyond a calender. SInce, Time Management is a very important skill that is often underlooked in schools, TYMO is supposed to help pick up the slack.


Journal

Journal is a place where you can reflect on your day and excpress your emotions, no matter what they are. No one besides you can see the journal, so you are ensured it doesn't leave your screen. However, you can only spend 15 minutes in the journal per day, so use your time wisely!

Calm Down

Calm Down is a feature that helps people cope with the pressures struggles of everyday living. We understand it can be difficult, so you can use this feature whenever you're stressed.


How We Help

TYMO takes many factors, such as your assignment, the amount of time you want to spend on the project and how much stuff you have left to do. This tool helps to teach people how to manage their time more effieciently.
