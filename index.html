Manage Your Stress
School is tough, and we get that, so we have some suggestions to keep your cool, no matter how demanding school can be.
Make sure you know what’s due, and when it’s due. Knowing this will give you peace of mind, it will prevent all-nighters, it will make unexpected surprises (and failures) much less likely.
Don’t spend too much time on one thing; Between school, extracurricular activities and leisure, we usually have a lot on our plate. This is fine, but don’t take extra classes or extra clubs that you don’t need or won’t enjoy.
Take short breaks in between study sessions. This can keep you calm by taking your mind off of the bigger picture for a second, no matter how scary it might be.
